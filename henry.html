King Henry VIII

By Patrick Eubanks, Tanner Reich, and Keegan Jurkiewicz

A Brief Biography

King Henry

Birth and Early Life

  • He was born on June 28, 1491, in Greenwich, London, England.
  • He had six siblings, but only three survived: Arthur, Margaret and Mary.
  • Arthur died at the age of 15.
  • This left Henry next in line for the kingship.
  • He was only 17 when he married Catherine, they were crowned king and queen. They later divorced in 1533.

Henry's Kingship

  • He was famously married 6 times.
  • The reason he married so many times in an attempt to have a male aire to the throne.
  • He had two of them beheaded for failing to provide him with a male aire.
  • He was the main instigator of the English reformation.
